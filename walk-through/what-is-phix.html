What is PHIX?

PHIX is a Personal Health Information eXchange, that can be deployed anywhere, providing an easy to use repository that individuals can use to store their health information, allowing for easy access by healthcare providers.

The goal behind PHIX is to provide a lightweight, open source solution that employs latest technology and open formats to empower individuals to take control over their healthcare information.

It is up to you as a developer and technology provider to deploy instances of PHIX that individuals can take advantage of. This site is mean to provide you with the information and resources you need to deliver on the PHIX vision and help individuals assert control over their healthcare data.
