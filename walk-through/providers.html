Providers

The providers group, has two simple endpoints, allowing you to add and get health care providers in the PHIX system. PHIX providers allow for healthcare practitioner access to the system, and open up the ability to access individualâ€™s health information contained PHIX deployments.

  • /providers (GET) - Retrieves a list of all healthcare providers that are currently in the PHIX system.
  • /providers (PUT) - Adds a new healthcare provider into the PHIX system.

You can access, and play with all the provider methods, using the PHIX interactive documentation.
